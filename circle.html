Circle Suits

Today, we're going to talk about the suits of Mahjong tiles.

Mahjong tiles have five main suits: Circle, Strip, Two Characters, Character, and Flower.

In this lesson, we'll focus on the Circle suit.

Although there are many different styles of Mahjong sets available.

All Circle tiles have one thing in common: they are made up of dots!

The number of dots on the tile represents the number of the tile.

From one to nine, there are nine different Circle tile faces in total.

Just like playing cards,

in a set of Mahjong tiles, there are four identical tiles for each Circle tile face.

So, there are 36 Circle tiles in total.

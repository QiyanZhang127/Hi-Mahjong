A-Character Suits

A-Character has 7 card suits.

Six of them are in one Chinese character, so it's called A-Characte.

Don't worry if you don't know the Chinese characters, for this set of tiles you don't need to know the words and meanings.

The remaining one is named empty and is a special card

It sometimes looks like a green door, sometimes it's blank.

Remember!

This green door, or a mahjong tile without a pattern, belongs to the Ace-Character group.

For A-Character tiles, you just need to remember that there are seven of them!

And remember! Cards of the same character love to stick together and they usually need to appear together.

So now! You just need to learn to find the same A- Character cards and put them together.

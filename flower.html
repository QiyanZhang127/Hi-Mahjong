Flower Suits

Flower suits will contain a picture (most of the time a flower) and a Chinese character.

This tile will not be used in most mahjong rules, it is used to keep score.

When you see it, just remove it from the pile, we don't need to use it!

If you're interested in this, you can learn how to play it after you've learnt the basics of mahjong
